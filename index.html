Immobilizer Software Development (Multiple OEM)

At Bosch, I engineer the immobilizer application software component, a critical security feature preventing unauthorized vehicle operation. This role involves deep engagement with cryptographic concepts, multicore processors (Infineon TC39x), and dedicated HSM cores to ensure robust vehicle security.

Key Contributions:
  • Architected and supported the complete product life cycle, from project acquisition and requirements engineering through to post-launch support.
  • Engineered secure cryptographic operations (encryption, decryption, TRNG, key management) by leveraging the ESCRYPT CycurHSM and its Cryptographic Service Abstraction Interface (CSAI).
  • Spearheaded requirements elicitation processes, resolving ambiguities and driving consensus among diverse stakeholders to establish clear, actionable project requirements and prevent rework.
